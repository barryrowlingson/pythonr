    A little LISP

    > (+ 1 2)
3

> (+ 6 (* 3 (- 8 12)))
-6

    R can be written a bit like this too!

    > `+`(1,2)
[1] 3
> `+`(6, `*`(3, `-`( 8, 12)))
[1] -6

    but not recommended, because R has "infix" notation:

    > (6 +  (3 * (8 - 12)))
[1] -6

    but sometimes infix isn't clear:

    > 6 + 3 * 8 - 12
[1] 18
